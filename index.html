King of the BIG Slice!

Stobie’s Pizza is a family owned and operated pizza restaurant in London, Ontario. Proudly owned and operated by owners, Bernie, John & Carol Stobie since 1997! We have been committed to quality food, friendly service and exceptional pricing!

Only The Freshest Ingredients!

Everything in our kitchen is brought in Fresh and we never reheat our food! In order to deliver a perfect quality product it has to start with nothing other than FRESH!

More Than Just Pizza!

Enjoy a great menu that includes Pizza, Donairs, Salads, Wings, Calzones & More.

FREE Pizza Slices Everyday!

Stobie's distinguishes itself in the pizzeria world through its pretty neat daily promotion. Each day, Stobies tweets a few first names on their Twitter account. If one of these first names happens to be yours, you can head into Stobies for a free slice of pizza!
